# R1 — Competitor Landscape (2026-05-06)

The "save-a-recipe-from-social-media" space has crowded fast since 2024. Most direct competitors now lean on LLM-driven extraction across IG Reels, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube Shorts, and arbitrary recipe URLs, with monetisation skewed toward freemium subscriptions ($3–$5/mo) plus a small free import quota. Below are 11 direct competitors spanning indie iOS-only apps to better-funded cross-platform plays.

## Notes on the field
- **Convergence on inputs.** Almost every entrant now supports the same four sources (IG, TikTok, Pinterest, generic URL). YouTube Shorts is becoming table-stakes; cookbook/handwritten OCR is the next differentiator (Flavorish, Crouton).
- **AI is no longer a moat.** "Caption + image + video frame + audio" extraction is being marketed by Pluck, Recipe Bro, Recipe Notes, and Pestle. Quality of structured output (scaling, units, substitutions) is the real battleground.
- **Pricing.** Two camps: freemium-with-import-cap (ReciMe 5/wk, Pestle Pro $3/mo) and free-forever-with-upsells (Recipe Notes, Cookmark). ReciGrams' offline-first + scaling angle is differentiated against ReciMe/Pluck.
- **Platform gap.** Pestle and Inspo are iOS-only; Cooking Guru is web-only. Cross-platform RN execution remains an opening.
