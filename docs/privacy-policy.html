Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Reci ("the app") is a personal recipe-saving app that lets you extract and store recipes from Instagram posts. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Instagram post URLs — entered by you to fetch recipe content. These are sent to the Facebook/Instagram oEmbed API to retrieve post metadata (thumbnail image, author name, and caption).
  • Recipe data — extracted from post captions or video frames using the Anthropic Claude API. This includes ingredients, instructions, and other recipe details.
  • API keys — if you enter a Claude API key in the app settings, it is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Instagram URLs are sent to Meta's oEmbed API solely to fetch post content.
  • Post captions or video frame images may be sent to the Anthropic Claude API to extract structured recipe data.
  • All saved recipes and app data are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram oEmbed API) — to retrieve public Instagram post metadata. Subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — to extract recipe information from post content. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

All recipe data is stored on your device. You can delete all data at any time from the app's Settings screen. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page.
